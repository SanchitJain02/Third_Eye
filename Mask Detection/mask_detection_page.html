About Mask Detection Module ?

During the Covid period, it was mandatory to wear masks to stop the spread of the Covid_19. Everywhere it was mandatory to wear a mask. At many places it was mandatory to wear a mask, and without a mask there was no entry. At such places, every person was checked manually and was told to wear a mask, but nobody has this much time to check each and every person face it there is a mask of not, so here this device can be useful as it will automatically tell who is wearing a mask and who is not wearing it. If a person tries to cover his or her face with hand, this device will show the percentage of the detection. If the percentage is less than 70% percent then that person is not wearing a mask and just covering his or her face with anything.
