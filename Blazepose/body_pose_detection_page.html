About Body Pose Detection ?

Many people suffer from back pain or any type of body pain. They are told to do exercise for the recovery of that injury or that diseases. Everyone donot know at what angel a person should perform an exercise, so they do it in a wrong way or in a very dangerous way. This project can help them by telling the angel of their body and warning them to be in limits while doing some kind of exercise. Suppose a person have to do a back stretch and donot know at what angel they should do it, this device will tell them when they are exceeding the maximum angle of safety.
